# Style: Comedy / Funny Moments

## Mood
Light-hearted, exaggerated, meme-worthy

## Typography
- Font: Bangers
- Size: 180-220px
- Tracking: 0 (standard — Bangers is naturally spaced)
- Horizontal scale: 100%
- Rotation: 3-7° (slight tilt)
- Case: ALL CAPS or Title Case

## Treatment
- Fill: Bright solid color (orange #FF6B35, red #FF4444, or pink #FF69B4)
- Outline: 10px, #1a1a2e
- Shadow: Hard drop, 8px/8px/0px, #000000 100%
- No gradient — flat, comic-style color

## Composition
- Text can be larger than usual (up to 35% of frame height)
- Can overlap the subject more aggressively
- Can be placed in unexpected positions

## Background
- Simple, not distracting
- Comic effects (impact lines, bursts, stars)
- High saturation

## Example Phrases
"BRUH"
"OOF"
"WAIT WHAT"
"BRO..."
"DESERVE"
