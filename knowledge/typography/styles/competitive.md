# Style: Competitive / Esports

## Mood
Intense, urgent, high-stakes

## Typography
- Font: Anton (or Lilita One narrowed to 105%)
- Size: 160-200px
- Tracking: -10 to -15 (tight)
- Horizontal scale: 100% (no stretch — Anton is already condensed)
- Case: ALL CAPS

## Treatment
- Fill: Solid white or solid gold (#FFD700)
- Outline: 8px, #1a1a2e
- Shadow: Long extrusion, 25px dark, 50% opacity
- No glow — clean and aggressive

## Composition
- Text in bottom third or top third
- Never center — offset opposite the subject

## Background
- Dark, high contrast
- Scanlines or grid overlay (10% opacity)
- Edge lighting

## Example Phrases
"R30 PUSH"
"RANK 1"
"UNSTOPPABLE"
"NEW RECORD"
