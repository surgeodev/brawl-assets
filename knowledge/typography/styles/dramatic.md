# Style: Dramatic / Cinematic

## Mood
Epic, emotional, legendary

## Typography
- Font: Lilita One
- Size: 140-180px
- Tracking: -5
- Horizontal scale: 108%
- Case: Title Case or Sentence case

## Treatment
- Fill: Gradient (warm gold to deep red, or icy blue to white)
- Outline: 6px, #1a1a2e
- Shadow: Soft ambient, 6px/8px/8px, #000000 70%
- Glow: Soft atmospheric glow behind text, matching fill color
- Extrusion: Soft, subtle (15px blur, 10px offset, 30% opacity)

## Composition
- Text in bottom third
- Generous negative space above text
- Text semi-transparently overlays the background

## Background
- Dark, atmospheric
- Light rays or lens flare from behind the subject
- Subtle fog or depth

## Example Phrases
"THE FINAL STAND"
"LEGENDARY"
"NEVER GIVE UP"
"ONE MORE TRY"
