# Glow Techniques for Brawl Stars Typography

## Atmospheric Backlight Glow
- Duplicate text layer
- Position behind main text
- Blur: 20-40px
- Color: Match the brawler's energy color
- Opacity: 30-50%
- Blend: Screen or Linear Dodge
- Effect: Makes text readable against any background

## Color Bleed Glow
- Duplicate text layer
- Position behind main
- Blur: 10-15px
- Saturation: +50 (intense color)
- Opacity: 60%
- Blend: Screen
- Effect: Neón / magical energy glow

## Soft Rim Light
- Duplicate text, scale 102-105%
- Blur: 2-4px
- Color: White or highlight color
- Opacity: 30%
- Effect: Subtle premium light rim around text edges

## Glow Anti-Patterns
- ❌ Glow that bleeds into adjacent text letters (use spacing)
- ❌ Glow that overpowers the fill color
- ❌ Multiple glow colors competing (stick to one)
- ❌ Glow without a corresponding light source in the composition
